Quesadilla

Description

Quesadilla with 500kcal only.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tortilla wrap
  • 100g jambon
  • Cheese
  • 50ml tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Parsley leaf

Steps

  1. Add 2 eggs
  2. Add tortilla wrap
  3. Add 100g jambon
  4. Add cheese
  5. Add the tomato sauce mixed with the onion and the parsley leaf
