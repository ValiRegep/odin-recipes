Pasta

Description

Pasta with 600kcal only.

Ingredients

  • 75g integral pasta
  • 100ml tomato sauce
  • 50g onion
  • 75g cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta
  2. Mix the cheese with the onion and the tomato sauce
  3. Put them on the pan and mix
  4. Add pasta upon the sauce and mix them
